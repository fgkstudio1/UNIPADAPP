#1 DECENTRALIZED LAUNCHPAD FOR MULTIVERSE

Unipad is a multi-chain, interoperable platform that can find and launch projects from any part of the crypto sphere. We are offering an exciting world of innovation through a multi-chain launchpad.

Unipad provides a unified crowdfunding platform for all things Substrate, giving future Binance Smart Chains ecosystem projects a means to bootstrap their development, grow their userbase, and fast-track their development.

What is UNIPAD Ecosystem

Multichain launchpad and Decentralized Incubator Protocol. Decentralizing seed funding and giving everyone early access to innovative projects.

As the crypto landscape has evolved to include more and more blockchains, most projects now have some degree of cross-chain focus on their roadmap — whether that be simply maintaining a token on multiple chains, or under assumption that they will eventually deploy their product on multiple platforms.

Unipad is borderless incubator for raising funds & scaling projects across the GameFi, NFT financing, DeFi and the entire Cryptocurrency Space.

Unipad born to provide safe and high potential projects to grow and reach them dream with Unipad Community. Also we want to provide safe and potentially profitable projects for our community. win win solution is our mission

The simple fact is, we have the resources, knowledge, and experience to deliver winning launchpad products — and Unipad will be no exception. All project must be safe, audit, KYC and AMA with Unipad Community.

The Power of Substrate

Speed

Substrate is fast. Plain and simple. Depending on their exact configuration, Substrate-based chains can achieve a throughput of potentially thousands of transactions per second (tps)

Security

All Kusama and Polkadot ecosystem projects are backed by the robust shared security of the relay chain

Efficiency

Both Kusama and Polkadot leverages the ultra energy-efficient nominated Proof-of-Stake consensus

Interoperability

Cross-parachain communication enables a whole range of use case that were not possible on previously isolated sovereign blockchains

Scaling

Capable of scaling to over 1 million tps, Polkadot and Kusama are able to support the mass market applications of tomorrow

Modularity

As an open-source ecosystem, Substrate provides builders with an entire suite of tools, modules, and battle-tested code that can be used to go from ideation to product in record time
